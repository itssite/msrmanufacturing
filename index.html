Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide a level of technical expertise that matches the rugged demands of the road. When you choose us, you are partnering with a team that knows every bolt, weld, and hydraulic circuit of a commercial vehicle. Advanced Diagnostics

Don't let a check engine light turn into a catastrophic failure. We provide Diesel truck repair and diagnostics that pinpoint issues within the ECM, fuel system, and transmission before they lead to a roadside breakdown.

Modern diesel engines are marvels of engineering, but they require a delicate touch and sophisticated software to maintain. At MSR, we invest heavily in the diagnostic tools required to communicate with today’s complex trucks. Whether you are running a late-model Freightliner or a vintage Peterbilt, we have the interface capabilities to read, interpret, and resolve error codes. This high-tech approach ensures that we aren't just throwing parts at a problem—we are fixing the root cause the first time.

Our repair philosophy extends beyond the engine bay. We look at the entire vehicle as a cohesive system. This includes the suspension, the braking system, the electrical harness, and the cooling systems. In the harsh Midwest climate, trucks face extreme temperature fluctuations and corrosive road treatments. Our team is specifically trained to identify environmental wear and tear, providing preventative maintenance that guards against the elements.

Engineered for the Long Haul

One of our proudest achievements is our specialized line of waste management equipment. We have perfected the process of Rolloff dumpster manufacturing, producing bins that are built to withstand the extreme stress of construction sites and industrial waste. Our dumpsters feature reinforced floor systems, heavy-duty rollers, and a paint finish that resists the corrosive nature of refuse. We don't just build them to look good—we build them to take a beating every single day.

The manufacturing process at MSR is a testament to American craftsmanship. We use high-tensile steel and advanced welding techniques to ensure that every seam is a bond of permanent strength. In the rolloff industry, failure at the hinge or the hook can be dangerous and costly. By controlling the manufacturing process in-house, we eliminate the weaknesses often found in mass-produced containers. This attention to detail is why our products are sought after by private contractors and municipal fleets alike.

But manufacturing is only half the story. Our facility is staffed by Heavy-duty truck repair specialists who understand how these specialized bodies interact with the truck's hydraulic system. This cross-disciplinary expertise means we can troubleshoot a malfunctioning hoist or a cracked frame with an eye for both the mechanical and the structural. We see the truck and the body as a single working tool, and we ensure they work in perfect harmony.
